Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

Introduction

Stackly is a browser extension that helps you organize your tabs into workspaces. We take your privacy seriously.

Data We Collect

What we collect

No personal data is collected or sent to external servers.

Stackly only stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Workspace names and configuration
  • URLs and titles of tabs saved in your workspaces
  • Your preferences such as theme and tab suspension settings
  • Local usage statistics such as workspace switches

What we do NOT collect

Personally identifiable information
Browsing history
Form data or passwords
Data sent to third parties
Tracking cookies
Behavioral analytics

Permissions Used

Permission Purpose
tabs Read and manage tabs so they can be saved into workspaces
storage Save your workspaces and preferences locally
alarms Trigger autosave and tab suspension logic

Data Storage

All data is stored using the chrome.storage.local API (or the Safari equivalent):

  • Your data stays only on your device
  • Your data is never synced to cloud servers
  • Your data is removed if you uninstall the extension

Data Sharing

Stackly does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Security

Because your data stays on your machine and is not transmitted externally, the privacy risk surface is intentionally limited.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us via the Stackly project repository.

Stackly respects your privacy by design.
